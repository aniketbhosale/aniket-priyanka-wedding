Our Love Story

First meet

"An invisble thread connects those who are destined to meet regardless of time,place,and circumstance.The thread may stretch or tangle. But it will never break."
This qoute fits perfect for Aniket and Priyanka's story. In 2011 Aniket and Priyanka meet each other for the first time at Infosys training in Mysore India. Very little did they have an idea that after 7 years 8431 miles on the other side of the world they would get hitched.

First date

"Where there's food there's hope" :D

The Proposal

"Don't wait for the perfect moment take the moment and make it perfect!"

Center of Whale Research

Center of Whale Research is dedicated towards the study and conservation of the Southern Resident killer whale (orca) population in the Pacific Northwest.Priyanka has adopted an Orca [Killer Whale] to support the conservation of the endangered species of the Southern Resident Killer Whale known as SRKW & also the member of the Whale museum in San Juan Island ,WA. Her role as a member of the Whale museum is to create awareness & the donations go to the research of the SRWK's. The SRKW's are basically of 3 pods (families) - J,K & L. The choice of diet for the SRKW is Chinook Salmon which is low in number causing food shortage which leads to malnutrition , miscarriage of breeding Orca's in turn leading to low numbers. Currently only 76 SRKW are alive and are mostly present in the following regions : SanJuan Islands ,WA in USA and Victoria BC & Vancouver in Canada. Donate here
